Background

Animal world a TV program, its main purpose is to introduce to the television audience all kinds of plants and animals in nature, so that the audience can not go out of the house to understand the earth's survival of all kinds of life, understand the impact of nature on human beings. In animal world, there is no didactical language. It tells and moves the audience through expert narration, beautiful pictures and touching stories, making the audience realize that we cannot live without animals. Indeed, the earth is a big home, because all the animals, the earth can keep the balance. With the rapid development of human beings, we should leave room for animals to live while we are building a beautiful home.

There are a lot of children like to see the animal world since childhood, they can learn about many different kinds of animals, and their living habits, also can make children learn to love animals from childhood. In today's society, there are many cases of killing animals at will, especially some children, they like to abuse cats, and often beat and scold some domestic animals. Although most of the animals in the animal world are wild animals, it can still make children have the heart of protecting animals to some extent.
